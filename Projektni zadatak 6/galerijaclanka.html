FPL experts: Is it time to stop captaining Haaland?


Some of the world's top managers on who they will hand the armband to over the next five Gameweeks

The Scout's panel of Fantasy Premier League experts give their thoughts on Erling Haaland (£14.0m) and how often they plan to captain the Manchester City star following his back-to-back blanks and ahead of a mixed run of fixtures.

Haaland is definitely a good captaincy option but perhaps not an autopick. Despite his recent form, he's still capable of producing multiple returns in any match. I'm captaining him against Brighton & Hove Albion and AFC Bournemouth and looking at Mohamed Salah (£12.6m) or Son Heung-min (£9.5m) as alternatives in the other weeks.

I still plan to captain Haaland in two of the next five Gameweeks - against Brighton and Bournemouth. Salah has great fixtures in the other three Gameweeks and he will be getting the armband then.

In the eight Gameweeks so far, I have moved the armband away from Haaland three times already, captaining Salah twice and James Maddison (£8.1m) once. Over the next 10 Gameweeks, I can only see myself captaining Haaland on three occasions.

10.10.2023.
