Greatness comes from everywhere

Premier League top goal scorers 2023-2024: Updated golden boot rankings as Erling Haaland targets own EPL record

After setting a new single-season Premier League goals record (36 goals) en route to the Golden Boot award in 2022/23, Manchester City star Erling Haaland has come out of the gates strong once again.

After just six matches, Haaland is already the leader in the Premier League Golden Boot rankings with eight goals. Following a hat-trick in City's 5-1 win over Fulham on September 2, Haaland added another in the 3-1 victory over West Ham and struck again against Nottingham Forest the following weekend.

It seemed that the Norwegian was about to pull away, but a few empty games have allowed Tottenham's Heung-min Son to pull within two, with Alexander Isak also there after scoring twice against West Ham. Hammers winger Jarrod Bowen is one behind that, as is Liverpool talisman Mohamed Salah whose double against Brighton saw him climb closer.

As was the case throughout the past two seasons, The Sporting News will update the top scorers' table below, with assists and minutes played serving as the first and second tiebreakers as per major European tournaments run by UEFA.
